iB Hubs

PAN INDIA STARTUP HUB

iB Hubs is a PAN India startup hub committed to developing a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in every nook and corner of India.

It provides end-to-end assistance to startups,giving equal importance to Purpose Driven Social Enterprises and NGOs as well as Profit Driven Enterprises.

With a vision to establish 2,500 centres across the country, iB Hubs has already established 30 centres across seven states, including five nodal centers in Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Lucknow.
